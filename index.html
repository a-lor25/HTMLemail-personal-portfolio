    About Me

    My name is A Lor. Yes, you read that right. My first name really is just 1 letter "A." I am a self-taught HTML Email Developer based out of Sun Prairie, WI eagered to enhance and develop more skills. I am ready to build beautiful, responsive emails for your clients!

    Skills

    My focus is on HTML Email Development. The skils that I have obtained are HTML, inling CSS, Responsive design, image editing by using GIMP to crop, edit, and compress images for quicker rendering, and email testing across various email clients using Putsmail. I use the code editor VS Code to write and edit my projects.
    My goal is to learn the MJML framework to increase productivity and efficiency when coding emails. Email Service Provider (ESP) such as MailerLite is also on my list to learn for email tracking, campaigns and automations.

    Education

    I obtained my Associates Degree at Fox Valley Techincal College in Accounting. I decided to study Email development after watching a few YouTube videos about Email and Web Development and started building small projects. I've always wondered as a boy growing up, asking myself: "How are these websites built? How can one create a fluid, responsive email and a beautiful, dynamic web? It felt great being able to build responsive emails and webpages and seeing the end result. Even though it was not an easy journey with many days of wanting to give up I kept going.
