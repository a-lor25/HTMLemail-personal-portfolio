    About Me

    My name is A Lor. Yes, you read that right. My first name really is just 1 letter "A." I am a self-taught Email Developer based out of Sun Prairie, WI eager to enhance and develop more skills. I am ready to build beautiful, responsive emails for your clients!

    Skills

    My focus is on Email Development. The skils that I have obtained are HTML, inling CSS, Responsive design, image editing by using GIMP to crop, edit, and compress images for quicker rendering, and email testing across various email clients using Putsmail. All of my projects were built from using the code editor VS Code.

    Goals

    My goal is to learn the MJML framework to increase productivity and efficiency when coding emails. Email Service Provider (ESP) such as Mailchimp is also on my list to learn for email tracking, campaigns and automations.

    How did I get into email development?

    I decided to study Email development after watching a few YouTube videos about Email and Web Development and started building small projects. I've always wondered as a boy growing up, asking myself: "How are these websites built? How can one create such a beautiful, dynamic web? I must be an Albert Enstein or Da Vinci in order to build something like this!" Coming to find out all it takes is discipline and motivation. It felt great being able to build responsive emails and seeing the end result. Even though it was not an easy journey with many days of wanting to give up I kept going and told myself "I can do it!". BUILD PROJECTS, KEEP PRACTICING! There are still many more to learn but trust the process!
